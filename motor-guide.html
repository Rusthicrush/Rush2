    How a DC Motor Works

    Motor working principle base — electromagnetism. Current-carrying conductor oru magnetic field-la vachirundha, adhukku oru force (thallal) kidaikum. Idha thaan Fleming's Left Hand Rule solllum.

    Current-carrying coil + magnetic field → rotational force (torque)

    PARTSDC Motor Anatomy

    • Stator Fixed part — permanent magnet or field coil, magnetic field create pannum
    • Rotor / Armature Rotating part — coil irukum, current pass aagum bothu force kidaikum
    • Commutator Rotor spin aagum bothu current direction-a switch pannum, continuous rotation ku
    • Brushes Carbon contacts — stationary circuit-la irundhu rotating commutator-ku current kadathum

    Current direction-a reverse pannina, motor direction-um reverse aagum — idhu thaan power window, wiper motor-la forward/reverse logic-oda base.

    Starter Motor — Auto Electrical Deep Dive

    Car/bike engine crank panna use aagura motor idhu — romba high torque, romba high current short duration ku venum (5-10 sec mattum run pannum, continuous run pannina burn aagidum).

    Starter circuit: thin trigger wire energizes solenoid → thick cable carries high current to motor

    FLOWStarting Circuit Sequence

    • 1. Key/button start → ignition switch-ku 12V send pannum
    • 2. Solenoid (relay) coil energize aagum — click sound idhu thaan
    • 3. Solenoid plunger push aagi, pinion gear-a flywheel ring gear-oda mesh pannum
    • 4. Same time-la solenoid main contacts close aagi, battery-la irundhu high current direct-a motor-ku pogum
    • 5. Motor spin aagi engine crank pannum, engine start aana key release, pinion disengage aagum

    Typical Draw

    Cranking current: 100–250A (car), stall/peak current start moment-la 300–600A+ varaikkum poidum.

    Why High Current?

    Stall/low-speed-la back EMF illa, adhanala resistance mattum current-a limit pannuthu — first instant-la current romba adhigam.

    Common Starter Motor Problems (Field Diagnosis)

    • Click sound, engine crank aagala — Weak battery, bad ground, or worn solenoid contacts
    • No sound at all — Dead battery, blown fuse, bad ignition switch, or open circuit
    • Slow/lazy crank — Weak battery, corroded terminals, worn brushes inside motor
    • Freewheels (spins but no crank) — Worn pinion gear or damaged flywheel ring gear teeth
    • Starter stays engaged after start — Stuck solenoid — dangerous, immediate-a disconnect pannunga

    Motor Formulas & Calculations

    P = T × ωPower = Torque × Angular velocity (rad/s)
    ω = 2πN / 60N = RPM
    P(watts) = V × IElectrical input power
    P(mech) = P(elec) × ηη = efficiency (0-1)
    I(stall) = V / R(winding)No back EMF at stall — max current
    Back EMF = V − I×RRises as motor speeds up, limits current
    T(Nm) = 9.55 × P(W) / N(rpm)Torque from power & RPM

    Motor Faults, Diagnosis & Testing

    FaultCauseFix / Prevention
    Worn brushesNormal wear over time (brushed motors)Replace brushes, clean commutator
    Burnt commutatorSparking, worn brushes, overheatingClean/resurface, replace if deep grooves
    Bearing noiseWear, lack of lubrication, dirt ingressGrease or replace bearings
    Winding burnt/shortOverload, stalled too long, overheatingRewind or replace motor
    Weak/no rotationWorn brushes, bad connections, weak supplyCheck voltage at motor, test brushes/commutator
    OverheatingOverload, blocked ventilation, low voltage runningCheck load, ensure proper voltage & airflow
    Solenoid click, no crankWorn solenoid contacts, weak batteryClean/replace solenoid contacts, load-test battery

    22.1Quick Field Tests

    • Voltage drop test Motor running-la, supply terminals-la voltage measure pannunga — expected-a vida romba kammi na, wiring/connection problem.
    • Continuity test Power off state-la, winding continuity multimeter-la check pannalam — open winding na burnt aagirukku.
    • Free-spin test Load illama hand-la spin panni paarunga — bearing noise/roughness theriyum.
    • Current draw test Clamp meter vechi running current measure pannunga — normal-a vida adhigama na, mechanical binding or winding issue.

    Motor Safety

    • ⚠ Starter motor test pannumbothu, gear engaged-a irukura vehicle-a neutral-la vekkanum — sudden movement risk.
    • ⚠ High current circuits (starter) work pannumbothu battery negative terminal-a first disconnect pannunga.
    • ⚠ Motor stall aagi irundha (locked rotor) continuous power kudukkatheenga — winding burn aagum, fire risk.
    • ⚠ Rotating parts (fan motor, pulley) near work pannumbothu loose clothing/fingers stay away.
    • ⚠ Motor casing hot-a irundha, touch pannuvadhukku munnadi power off panni cool aaga vidunga.
    • ⚠ Insulated tools use pannunga, short-circuit spark risk kammi pannum.
