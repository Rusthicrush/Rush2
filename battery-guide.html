    Electrochemistry — Eppadi Work Aaguthu

    Battery oru chemical energy storage device. Ulla irukura chemicals reaction aagum bothu, electrons free aaguthu. Andha electrons oru electrode-la irundhu (Anode) veliya wire vazhiya poyi, matha electrode-ku (Cathode) pogum. Indha electron flow-a thaan electric current nu solrom.

    DISCHARGE: chemical energy → electrical energy

    3 PARTSBattery Anatomy

    • Anode (−) Negative terminal. Oxidation reaction nadakkura place, electrons idhula irundhu velila varum.
    • Cathode (+) Positive terminal. Reduction reaction nadakkura place, electrons idhuku vandhu serum.
    • Electrolyte Ions-a oru electrode-la irundhu vera electrode-ku move panna help pannum, aana electrons-a direct-a pass panna vidathu.

    Discharging

    Battery current-a velila kuduthu, device-a run pannurathu. Chemical → Electrical.

    Charging

    Velila irundhu current push panni, reaction-a reverse pannurathu. Electrical → Chemical. (Rechargeable types ku mattum.)

    Battery Chemistries — Deep Dive

    4.1Lead-Acid (Car / Bike Battery)

    Daily work pannura car/bike battery Lead-Acid thaan. 6 cells irukum, ovvoru cell ~2.1V. Series-la connect panna 6 × 2.1 = 12.6V (approx "12V").

    • Anode Lead (Pb)
    • Cathode Lead Dioxide (PbO₂)
    • Electrolyte Dilute Sulfuric Acid (H₂SO₄ + Water)

    Discharge aagum bothu, rendu plates um Lead Sulfate (PbSO4) aaga maarum, electrolyte acid concentration kammi aagum — adhanala discharged battery specific gravity kammi irukum (hydrometer vechi check pannalam).

    12V pack — 6 cells in series (illustrative, 5 shown)

    4.2NiCd / NiMH

    Old-time rechargeable AA batteries. NiCd-la cadmium toxic-a irukurathala, ippo NiMH thaan popular. Memory effect problem konjo irukku — full discharge pannama repeated partial charge pannina capacity kammi aana feeling varum (Li-ion ku illa).

    4.3Lithium-Ion (Li-ion) — Phone / Laptop

    Lead-acid ah vida light weight, adhe energy-a small size-la store pannalam.

    • Anode Graphite (Carbon) layer
    • Cathode Lithium Metal Oxide (LiCoO₂ / LiFePO4 type)
    • Separator Porous plastic film, short-circuit varamal thadukkum
    • Electrolyte Lithium salt solution

    4.4LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate)

    Li-ion oda safer version. Voltage konjo kammi (3.2V/cell), aana thermal runaway risk romba kammi, cycle life romba adhigam (2000–5000 cycles). Solar banks, EV, e-bike heavy-duty ku best choice.

    05

    Charging Method — CC-CV

    Li-ion battery-a correct-a charge panna CC-CV (Constant Current – Constant Voltage) method use pannuvanga.

    CC-CV curve — cyan: voltage rises · amber: current tapers

    Stage 1 — CC

    Fixed current (e.g. 1A) kudukum, voltage slowly rise aagum 3.0V → 4.2V varaikkum.

    Stage 2 — CV

    Voltage 4.2V-la fix pannitu, current gradually taper aagum, full aana automatic cut aagum.

    Lead-Acid — Different Method

    Bulk Charge (high current) → Absorption Charge (constant ~14.4V) → Float Charge (trickle, ~13.2–13.8V, full-a irundha maintain pannurathukku).

    06

    Series vs Parallel Connections

    Battery packs (power bank, e-bike, inverter bank) design pannurathukku key concept idhu.

    Vtotal = V1+V2+V3...Series — capacity same as single cell
    Ahtotal = Ah1+Ah2+Ah3...Parallel — voltage same as single cell
    • ⚠ Series/Parallel connect pannurappo same voltage, same capacity, same age/health cells thaan use pannanum. Mismatch cells connect pannina weak cell over-stress aagi damage aagum, fire risk-um varum.

    6.3Mixed Series-Parallel

    E-bike, laptop, power tool packs-la rendu combination um use pannuvanga — "NSxMP" notation. Example: 4S4P = 4 cells series (voltage ×4), andha string 4 groups parallel (capacity ×4).

    Real-Life Worked Examples

    Backup/runtime comparison across the 4 sizing examples below (*e-bike shown per-charge, not directly comparable scale)

    1. Car Battery Accessory Load

    Fog lamps (5A) + stereo (3A), engine off. Battery: 60Ah.

    Total current = 5A + 3A8A
    Backup time = 60Ah / 8A7.5 hrs
    ≈ 7.5 hours theoretical (real-la konjo kammi, age/temp factor)

    2. Inverter Battery Backup Time

    150W load, 150Ah/12V battery, inverter efficiency 85%.

    Load current = 150W / 12V12.5A
    Adjusted = 12.5 / 0.8514.7A
    Backup = 150Ah / 14.7A~10.2 hrs
    ≈ 10.2 hours backup

    3. E-Bike Pack Sizing (4S4P)

    18650 cells, 3.7V / 2500mAh each, need 14.8V system.

    Series count = 14.8V / 3.7V4 (4S)
    Total voltage = 4 × 3.7V14.8V
    Total capacity = 4 × 2500mAh10,000mAh
    Total energy = 14.8V × 10Ah148Wh
    4S4P → 14.8V, 10Ah, 148Wh

    4. Solar Battery Bank Sizing (Village Setup)

    2 LEDs (10W each) + 1 fan (40W) = 60W, 5 hrs/night, 12V system.

    Daily energy = 60W × 5hrs300Wh
    Required Ah = 300Wh / 12V25Ah
    +30% safety margin~32.5Ah
    Minimum 35Ah battery vechi design pannalam

    5. Phone Battery Charging Time

    4000mAh phone, 2A charger (CC phase mattum, simple calc ku).

    Time = 4.0Ah / 2A2 hrs
    CC phase 2 hrs; real-la CV phase add aagi 2.5–3 hrs aagum

    10

    Advanced: Peukert's Law

    Lead-acid battery-la oru interesting effect irukku — high current-la fast-a discharge pannina, effective capacity kammi aagidum (rated Ah full-a kidaikadhu). Starter motor mathiri high current draw application-ku battery sizing pannum bothu idhu consider pannanum.

    Peukert effect: higher current draw = less usable capacity

    Adhanala thaan battery Ah rating-a solrappo "20-hour rate" nu specify pannuvanga (e.g. 100Ah @ 20hr rate = 5A for 20 hours). Fast discharge (starter motor, 100A+) la andha same battery 100Ah kudukadhu — 55–60Ah mattum thaan real-a kidaikum.

    11

    Battery Management System (BMS)

    Li-ion battery packs-la (phone, e-bike, power bank) BMS oru circuit board irukum, idhu battery-a protect pannum. Illa na, over-charge aana fire risk, over-discharge aana permanent damage.

    BMS sits between the pack and the output — protects both directions
    • Over-charge 4.2V mela pogama fixed cut pannum
    • Over-discharge 3.0V kku keela pogama cut pannum
    • Over-current/short sudden high current-a cut pannum
    • Cell balancing series cells ellame same voltage-la irukka balance pannum
    • Temp monitoring over-heat aana charge/discharge stop pannum

    Common Faults, Diagnosis & Testing

    Auto electrical work ku romba useful section.

    FaultCauseFix / Prevention
    SulfationLong time discharged state-la vachirundhaDesulfator charger, avoid deep discharge
    Terminal corrosionAcid fumes + moistureBaking soda + water clean, grease apply
    Swelling (Li-ion)Over-charge, heat damage, internal shortReplace immediately, don't puncture
    Thermal runawayInternal short + heat chain reactionNever charge damaged/swollen cells
    Capacity lossAge, cycle count, heat exposureNormal aging — replace after cycle life
    Won't hold chargeInternal short, dead cell in packLoad test / cell replacement
    Slow crank (car)Weak battery or bad connectionLoad test, check terminals/cables

    12.1Testing Methods

    • Multimeter Resting voltage check, quick SoC idea kidaikum.
    • Load test Known load kudukkumbothu voltage evlo drop aaguthu paakanum. Healthy battery-la drop kammi.
    • Hydrometer (Lead-acid) Specific gravity check — full ~1.265, discharged ~1.120.
    • Capacity test Full charge → known current-la discharge → real capacity theriyum.
    • Internal resistance meter Battery health quick-a check panna (pro tools).
    13

    Battery Safety

    • ⚠ Battery-a short-circuit pannatheenga — sudden high current, spark, fire risk irukku.
    • ⚠ Li-ion battery-a puncture/crush pannatheenga — thermal runaway aagi fire varum.
    • ⚠ Lead-acid charge pannumbothu hydrogen gas produce aagum — ventilated area-la, spark/flame illama vechi charge pannunga.
    • ⚠ Different chemistry/age/brand batteries-a series/parallel-la kalakathaenga.
    • ⚠ Swollen/damaged Li-ion cells-a charge pannatheenga, immediate-a safe-a dispose pannunga.
    • ⚠ Terminals short aagama, insulated tools use pannunga.
    • ⚠ Extreme heat (sunlight, exhaust near) la battery vaikatheenga — life kammi, safety risk adhigam.

    Future of Batteries

    Solid-State

    Solid electrolyte, safer (no leak/fire risk), higher energy density — innum expensive, mass production varala.

    Sodium-ion

    Lithium-ku alternative, cheap raw material, aana energy density konjo kammi.

    Silicon Anode

    Graphite-ku badhila silicon use panni capacity adhigama increase panna try pannuranga.

    Faster Charging

    Graphene-based, silicon-carbon hybrid anodes — 10-15 min full charge target.
